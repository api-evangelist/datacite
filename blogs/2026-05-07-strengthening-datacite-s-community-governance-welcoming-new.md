We are pleased to announce the results of the 2026 DataCite Board elections, which were confirmed during last week’s annual Business Meeting of the General Assembly. Along with the DataCite membership, DataCite’s Board plays a central role in supporting robust community governance, global representation, and collaborative stewardship of open research infrastructure. Individuals from DataCite member organizations are eligible to stand for election.
